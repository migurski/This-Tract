This Tract is a view into U.S. 2000 Census data for every tract, built in anticipation of the forthcoming 2010 Census data release. It uses your web browser’s built-in geolocation feature to give you a view of the demographics of your local area, or you can search by address or location. Pretty much everything here assumes a modern browser like Firefox, Safari, or Chrome. Geographic lookup by FCC’s Census Block Conversions API. Tract outlines from U.S. Census shapefiles. Demographic data from U.S. 2000 Census by way of census-tools. Map tile imagery courtesy of MapQuest and OpenStreetMap. Pie charts by Google Charts. Slippy maps by Modest Maps JS. Browser geolocation bits from YQL Geo Library by way of Max Wheeler. This Tract is a thing by Stamen Design’s Michal Migurski, and it can be found on Github.
